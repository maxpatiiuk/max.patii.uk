This website was developed in 2019 for my parent's shop. It was hosted on
[socksy.in.ua](http://socksy.in.ua)

It is a landing page that was linked to from printed and online ads.

The main purpose of the site was brand awareness and providing further contact
details (physical address, Facebook, Instagram, YouTube, phone number, Email).

This website has since been retired. The URL is now redirecting to our
[Facebook page](https://facebook.com/socksy.linen/). We also purchased a shorter
domain [socksy.in.ua](https://socksy.in.ua) that also leads to
[Facebook](https://facebook.com/socksy.linen/).

Facebook is going to be used over this website going forward because:

- Facebook is a popular social media among our target audience
- It is easier to update than the website
- It has easy options for contacting us (Facebook Messenger)
- It integrates nicely with our Instagram page
- It provides easily accessible stats
